Krishna Trust - User Guide & Functionality Documentation

1. Introduction

What is the Krishna Trust Project?
Krishna Trust is a comprehensive web application built for a charitable organization. It helps the trust manage its day-to-day activities efficiently while providing a transparent platform for the public to learn about their work and contribute.

Who is this for?

  • Public Users / Donors: Can view the trust's work, read testimonials, see impact photos, and make online donations.
  • Administrators: Can log into a secure backend to manage beneficiaries (Labharthis), record donations, notify past donors, track employee attendance, manage expenses, and generate detailed reports.

2. Public Website (For General Users)

The public website is open to everyone and does not require a login.

Step-by-Step Process:

  1. Home Page: Open the website URL. Here, you will see an overview of the trust's services, recent impact photos, and testimonials.
  2. Making a Donation: On the Home page, locate the Donation Form. Fill in your details (Name, Mobile, Address, Amount, PAN, etc.), select the payment mode, and submit the form to record your donation.
  3. Viewing Impacts (Gallery): Click on the Impact menu. You can view photos of the trust's charitable activities categorized by month.
  4. Contacting the Trust: Go to the Contact page and fill out the inquiry form with your message, which will be sent directly to the administrators.

3. Admin Panel (For Administrators)

The Admin Panel is a secure area for managing the trust's operations.

Step-by-Step Process:

A. Logging In & Dashboard

  • Go to the /login page.
  • Enter your Admin Email and Password, or Sign in with Google if your Google account has admin access.
  • Upon successful login, you will see the Dashboard, displaying a quick summary of Total Donations, Beneficiaries, and Uploaded Media.
  • Language Preferences: You can switch the portal language between English and Gujarati to suit your preference.

B. Beneficiary (Labharthi) Management

Labharthis are the people receiving aid (like tiffin services) from the trust.

  • View: Go to the Labharthi section to see a list of all beneficiaries.
  • Add New: Click "Add Labharthi". Fill in their details. A unique Labharthi Number (e.g., RJ0001) will be auto-generated.
  • Edit/Delete: Use the Action buttons to update or remove records.
  • Reorder: Use the drag-and-drop feature to change the display position of beneficiaries.

C. Donation Management

  • Go to the Donation section to view all received donations.
  • Add Manual Donation: Click "Add Donation" to record cash or cheque donations.
  • Receipt Generation: Click the receipt button next to a donation to generate a digital receipt in Gujarati.
  • WhatsApp Sharing: Click the WhatsApp icon to instantly send the donation receipt to the donor's mobile number.

D. Notify Past Donors

  • Navigate to the Notify Donor section.
  • The system lists donors who contributed in the previous years (1 or 2 years ago) during the current month but haven't donated yet this year.
  • Mark as Done: After sending them a reminder message (e.g. via WhatsApp), click "Mark as Notified" to remove them from the pending list.

E. Attendance Tracking

  • Navigate to the Attendance section.
  • Mark daily attendance for active beneficiaries as Present, Absent, or Not Specified.

F. Employee Management

  • Go to the Employee section to manage staff details and salaries.
  • Withdrawals: Record partial salary withdrawals. The system automatically calculates their remaining final salary.

G. Media Gallery (Content)

  • Go to the Contents section to upload new photos of recent charitable events. These will appear on the public "Impact" page.

H. Expense Tracking

  • Go to the Expense section to record operational expenses and keep accounting up to date.

I. Monthly Reports

  • Navigate to Monthly Reports.
  • Select a module and choose a Month/Year to download a formatted Excel Report.

J. Leaflet Map

  • Go to the Map section to view an interactive map displaying the geographical locations of all beneficiaries.
